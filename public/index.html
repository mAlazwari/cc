DISCOVERY #01

×

The Mars Cats cautiously approached a mysterious, metal prominence conspicuously peeking out from the umber soil. This was one of their regular red planet reconnaissance missions, yet their curiosity was piqued by this unforeseen discovery; a hatch. Then a sense of foreboding washed over them as a countdown clock ticking ominously was protruding from the terrain closely nearby. The eerie atmosphere intensified when a familiar, yet cryptic word was observed scrawled hastily next to the hatch upon a martian boulder; “ICARIA.” Overwhelmed by what they had stumbled across, the confounded kitties stared at the puzzle trying to put the pieces together. But they would reach no satisfying resolutions… yet. However, they were resolved that what lay beyond the bulkhead held secrets that could alter the course of their entire mission. The cats quickly snap some pictures and hasten back to HQ, eager to inform the leadership of their perplexing discovery. Yet, as they relay the details, the sense of confusion and unease settles over the room as it did hours earlier in the field. No one knows quite what to make of it all – how could this hatch exist, bearing a word that was forbidden from use during their mission? Speculation runs rampant as they ponder the significance of "ICARIA" scrawled beside the mysterious doorway, its meaning lost to them. And the countdown clock; countdown to what?
